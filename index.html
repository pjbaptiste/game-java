ROCK - PAPER - SCISSOR

Let's play to the Console

**How to play**

  1. press F12 or ctrl+shift+i or right click on the page and click inspect
  2. click on the console tab
  3. and now type play()
  4. in the modal windows, write: rock or paper or scissor to make your choice
    • Note: if you write something else that is not "rock-paper-scissor", the program will consider you lost.
  5. the program will ask you between 5 to 10 times to make your choice, everything will depend on the score.
  6. Whoever has 5 points before, will be the winner...

Enjoy yourself...
